What is ZingIt?

ZingIt is an open source UI marker tool created by Dan Wahlin that lets developers and designers point at elements on a webpage, describe what changes they want, and send those instructions directly to AI coding agents for automated implementation.

Instead of writing detailed prompts about UI elements to change, creating screenshots, and pasting all of the details into an agent, you simply click on what you want to change and describe the fix concisely. ZingIt will even generate a screenshot for you to send to the agent automatically!

How It Works

1. Point & Click

Simply click any element on your webpage. ZingIt instantly captures its CSS selector, HTML structure, and visual context‚Äîno manual inspection needed.

2. Describe Your Vision

Write what you want in plain English. "Make this button blue and larger", "Add hover effects", "Fix the alignment"‚ÄîZingIt understands natural language.

3. Watch AI Build It

Send to your AI agent (Claude, Copilot, or Codex) and watch as it locates the exact code, makes precise changes, and implements your vision automatically.

Visual Context Matters

Automatically captures pixel-perfect screenshots of marked elements. Give AI agents the visual context they need to understand design intent and implement changes accurately.

Works Everywhere
